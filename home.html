ABOUT OUR UNIVERSITY

Experience Excellence at
Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University

Established in 1998, GGSIPU is a dynamic ecosystem of teaching and research excellence, dedicated to creating professionals who lead with integrity.

To provide market-oriented professional education and establish Centers of Excellence in technical and professional education to meet the needs of Indian industries.

"Jyotivṛṇīta Tamaso Vijānan" - To stimulate the hearts and minds of scholars, empowering them to contribute to the welfare of society and the nation.

Fostering a culture of innovation, integrity, transparency, and accountability across all 18 Schools of Study and affiliated institutions.

  • NAAC 'A++' Accredited University
  • Ranked 80th in NIRF 2024
  • 111+ Affiliated Institutes of Excellence
  • State-of-the-Art Research Facilities
